Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy but has become a worldwide favorite. It typically consists of a thin, crispy crust topped with tomato sauce and melted cheese, along with a variety of toppings such as meats, vegetables, and herbs. The combination of flavors and textures creates a satisfying and comforting meal that can be enjoyed by people of all ages. Pizza is a versatile dish that can be customized to suit individual tastes and preferences. Whether you prefer a classic Margherita pizza or a loaded meat lover's pie, there's a pizza out there for everyone. It's a go-to food for parties, family dinners, and even quick lunches or snacks.

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out pre-made pizza dough onto a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edge.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add desired toppings.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
