Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is beloved by many. Its layers of pasta, cheese, and meat sauce make it a hearty and satisfying meal that is perfect for any occasion. It's a dish that can be customized to your liking by adding in different ingredients or swapping out the meat for a vegetarian option. Whether you make it from scratch or use a pre-made sauce, lasagna is a versatile and delicious dish that is sure to please. Lasagna is also a great dish for meal prep and leftovers. It can easily be reheated in the oven or microwave and makes for a quick and satisfying meal when you're short on time. You can also freeze lasagna for later, making it a convenient option for busy weeknights. With its rich and comforting flavors, lasagna is a classic dish that has stood the test of time and will continue to be a favorite for years to come.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles and brown the meat with onions and garlic.
  2. Make the tomato sauce and cheese mixture.
  3. Layer the noodles, cheese mixture, and sauce in a baking dish.
  4. Bake in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 50-55 minutes.
  5. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before serving.
