Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian dish that consists of spaghetti noodles served with a hearty meat-based sauce. The sauce is typically made with ground beef, tomatoes, onions, garlic, and a variety of herbs and spices, which are cooked together to create a rich and flavorful sauce. The spaghetti noodles are boiled until they are tender and then tossed with the sauce, creating a delicious and satisfying meal that is perfect for any occasion. The combination of tender spaghetti noodles and a savory meat-based sauce makes spaghetti bolognese a favorite dish for many. It's a hearty and comforting meal that can be enjoyed any time of year, and it's easy to customize to suit your tastes. Whether you're a fan of spicy food or prefer a milder flavor, you can adjust the ingredients to create a spaghetti bolognese that is perfect for you.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Garlic
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti noodles according to package directions and set aside.
  2. Brown ground beef with onions and garlic in a pan.
  3. Add canned tomatoes, tomato paste, and seasonings to the pan.
  4. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Serve sauce over spaghetti noodles and enjoy!
